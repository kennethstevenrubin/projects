The Voyager

 
 Relief does not exist throughout eternity,
 Eternity's grounds are for reality to decree,
 Yet there are those who would have no bounds,
 And their capsules set them free.
 
 Be. Yet head for the heavenly--
 Though Earth-bound eventually.
 
 In the altered state, listen!
 For the fantastic appear,
 And the invisible are here.
 
 Yet in the suffering of those who wake,
 Reality is all that's left,
 To the the voyager...and the man,
 And the man's mind...bereft!
 
 

Heart

 
 usually it happens after a brief summer rain...
 in the morning they arrive, with the
 dew and fog
 
 it's the heavy odor of life that stirs them, for
 the air is thick and rich with green
 
 always they move among the trees and bushes,
 darting into the dark and cover
 
 that is not to say the fields are bare! for
 they go there too, to skip and scatter like hares
 
 yes, you have seen them -- though perhaps not
 for what they were
 
 they have been about since the fields were
 new, and the forests drank in mystery
 
 for their ways are the old ways
 the ways of earth and mud and stone
 
 and we are but passengers to them
 always awake and always alone
 
 so go slow, speak to trees, take long
 forgotten walks; and they will be with you
 to keep you young at heart

What It Is To Be Alive

 
 I feel at one with with world this morning.
 As people race and struggle to get to work,
 I don't feel this as the separation of ego but rather
    the community of beings being alive--
    the breath of the universe surges through us all.
 
 What we want is what all beings want.
 What we know, all beings know.
 It is the never-ending striving
 The seed reaching up through soil
 The solitary bird embracing sky.
 
 For there is no separation.
 Incarnation itself brings us together
    So that we might spark and diminish
    So that we might feel what it is to be alive.
